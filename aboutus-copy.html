WHO ARE WE?

We are are a small and cozy Middle Eastern restaurant located in the heart of Aarhus. It all started when we were one day looking around Aarhus for a restaurant to sit down and enjoy a delicious kebab. After the realisation that such a place did not exist, we made a conclusion that something needed to change. The potential we saw combined with our passion for Middle Eastern food lead to us opening our dearest Shawarma King. We are a must visit restaurant if you want an authentic Middle-Eastern experience in Aarhus.

We offer a variety dishes from the Middle east. From Shawarma to Durum, we have it all. Our kitchen houses the most talented cooks with years of experience with Middle Eastern quisine. We use the freshest ingredients imported directly from countries such as Lebanon to ensure that every time you visit us you experience the authentic taste of Middle Eastern fast-food.
